Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Overview

Munjez is an offline-first desktop app focused on productivity. We designed it to keep your data local whenever possible.

Data We Collect

We do not require account creation to use Munjez, and we do not collect personal data from inside the app by default.

Local Data Storage

Your tasks, habits, and app settings are stored locally on your device. You are responsible for managing backups and device security.

Crash Reports and Diagnostics

Munjez does not automatically send analytics or diagnostics unless clearly stated in a future release note and explicitly enabled by you.

Third-Party Links

Our website and app may include links to third-party websites (such as GitHub). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Munjez is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at munjez.support@proton.me.
